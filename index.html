Main Characters

Goku

Goku is the main protagonist of Dragon Ball Z. A Saiyan raised on Earth, he fights to protect his loved ones and the planet. Goku constantly pushes his limits and achieves the legendary Super Saiyan form.

Vegeta

The Prince of Saiyans, Vegeta starts as Goku's rival but later becomes one of Earth’s defenders. He is driven by pride and a desire to surpass Goku, eventually transforming into a Super Saiyan.

Frieza

Frieza is one of the most feared villains in the universe, known for his ruthlessness and quest for immortality. His battle with Goku on Planet Namek is one of the most intense fights in Dragon Ball Z history.

Piccolo

Originally a villain, Piccolo evolves into one of Goku's greatest allies. As a Namekian warrior, Piccolo trains Gohan and plays a critical role in protecting Earth from powerful foes.

Gohan

Gohan, the eldest son of Goku, is a powerful warrior with immense potential. Though he prefers a peaceful life, Gohan’s hidden power is unleashed during the Cell Saga when he becomes the first to achieve Super Saiyan 2.

Trunks

Trunks is the son of Vegeta and Bulma, and he hails from a future ravaged by the Androids. He travels back in time to warn the Z Fighters of the impending threat, becoming a key figure in the Android and Cell Sagas.

Bulma

One of Goku's oldest friends, Bulma is a brilliant scientist and the creator of many of the Z Fighters' advanced technologies. She plays a key role in supporting the team throughout their battles.

Krillin

Krillin is one of Goku's best friends and one of Earth's strongest humans. Despite his lack of Saiyan strength, his courage and quick thinking have made him a key player in several major battles.

Cell

Cell is a bio-engineered android created by Dr. Gero. His goal is to achieve perfection by absorbing Androids 17 and 18. He becomes one of the most dangerous foes the Z Fighters have ever faced during the Cell Games.

Majin Buu

Majin Buu is an ancient, powerful entity capable of destruction on a massive scale. Despite his childlike behavior, his strength and transformations make him one of the most formidable villains in Dragon Ball Z.

Dragon Ball Z Sagas

Saiyan Saga

The Saiyan Saga introduces Goku's Saiyan heritage and the arrival of Raditz, Vegeta, and Nappa. Earth's heroes face off against these powerful foes in a battle that determines the planet's fate.

  • Goku learns he's a Saiyan.
  • Vegeta and Nappa's arrival.
  • Goku’s sacrifice and training in the afterlife.
  • Goku vs. Vegeta showdown.

Namek Saga

Gohan, Krillin, and Bulma travel to Planet Namek in search of the Dragon Balls to revive their friends. However, they must face the evil Frieza, who seeks immortality through the Dragon Balls.

  • Planet Namek exploration.
  • Frieza’s search for immortality.
  • Goku's arrival after healing from injuries.

Frieza Saga

The Frieza Saga sees Goku’s epic battle against Frieza on Planet Namek. Goku achieves the legendary Super Saiyan transformation during the intense battle, marking a turning point in Dragon Ball Z.

  • Goku becomes a Super Saiyan.
  • Frieza’s multiple transformations.
  • Destruction of Planet Namek.

Android Saga

The Android Saga introduces new threats created by Dr. Gero, targeting Goku for revenge. Trunks from the future warns the Z Fighters, leading to their preparation for the powerful androids.

  • Arrival of Future Trunks.
  • Introduction of Androids 17, 18, and 16.
  • Goku's heart virus.

Cell Saga

The Cell Saga centers on the bio-engineered being Cell, who absorbs the androids to become perfect. Gohan surpasses Goku in strength, achieving Super Saiyan 2 and defeating Cell in the Cell Games.

  • Cell's evolution into his Perfect Form.
  • Gohan becomes Super Saiyan 2.
  • The Cell Games and Cell’s defeat.

Majin Buu Saga

Majin Buu, an ancient evil, threatens the universe. Goku, Vegeta, and the Z Fighters face the destructive force of Buu, leading to Goku’s transformation into Super Saiyan 3 and the final battle.

  • Majin Buu's transformation and destruction.
  • Goku's Super Saiyan 3 transformation.
  • Final battle with Kid Buu and the Spirit Bomb.

Fusion Saga

In the Fusion Saga, Goku and Vegeta fuse into Vegito to stop Super Buu. The Z Fighters use powerful fusion techniques to confront the immense power of Buu.

  • Goten and Trunks fuse into Gotenks.
  • Goku and Vegeta fuse into Vegito.
  • Super Buu’s absorption of fighters.

Kid Buu Saga

The final battle in the Kid Buu Saga pits Goku and Vegeta against Kid Buu, the purest form of Buu. Goku defeats Kid Buu with the Spirit Bomb, finally ending Buu’s reign of terror.

  • Kid Buu's destruction of Earth.
  • Goku vs. Kid Buu on the Supreme Kai's planet.
  • The Spirit Bomb ends Kid Buu.
